CSS BOX MODEL

Box Model :

XYZ

  • Border area
    • it is the area between box padding and margin its dimensions are given by width and height of border
  • Margin area
    • it is the area consists of space between border and margin
  • Padding area
    • it includes elements of padding this area is actually space around the content area within border box
  • Content area
    • the area consists of contents like text image or other mediaand its dimendsions is given content box and width and height
